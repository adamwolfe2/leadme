## Key Metrics to Monitor

1. **Scroll Depth**: How far visitors scroll (target: 75%+ reach comparison table)
2. **Calculator Engagement**: % who interact with ROI calculator
3. **FAQ Clicks**: Which objections are most common
4. **Tier Selection**: Conversion rate by tier (expect Outbound highest)
5. **Annual vs. Monthly**: % choosing annual (target: 60%+)
6. **CTA Click Rate**: Primary vs. secondary CTAs
7. **Time on Page**: Should increase with interactive elements

---

## Mobile Considerations

- Pricing cards stack vertically
- Calculator slider is touch-friendly
- FAQ accordions work well on mobile
- CTAs are thumb-sized
- Table scrolls horizontally on small screens

---

## A/B Test Ideas

1. **Anchor Order**: Pipeline first vs. Data first
2. **Annual Discount**: 20% vs. 17% vs. 25%
3. **Calculator Position**: Before vs. after pricing cards
4. **Badge Copy**: "Most Popular" vs. "Best Value" vs. "Recommended"
5. **CTA Copy**: "Get Started" vs. "Start Outbound" vs. "Book Demo"
6. **Testimonial Format**: Text vs. video
7. **FAQ Order**: Most clicked first vs. logical flow
