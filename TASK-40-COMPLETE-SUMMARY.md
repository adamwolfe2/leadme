## Seven Sweeps Framework Applied to ALL Posts

### ✅ 1. Automated Rebranding
- **Retarget IQ** → **Cursive** (all instances)
- **retargetiq.com** → **meetcursive.com**
- Footer sections removed (Recent Posts, "How Retarget IQ Drives Results")
- Competitor product references removed
- Source URLs removed

### ✅ 2. Clarity Sweep
- Jargon simplified: "leverage" → "use", "facilitate" → "help"
- Sentences kept under 25 words
- Concrete Cursive examples added throughout
- Paragraphs made scannable (2-4 sentences max)
- Technical terms explained when first used

### ✅ 3. Voice & Tone Sweep
- Professional yet human voice throughout
- Removed overly casual "we all" language
- Added technical authority with specific features
- Strengthened confidence (removed "might," "almost," "quite")
- Active voice used consistently

### ✅ 4. "So What" Sweep
- Features converted to outcomes
- Added "which means..." bridges throughout
- Quantified with Cursive stats: 70%, 220M+, 140M+, 450B+, 200+
- Connected every feature to reader pain points
- Benefit-focused instead of feature-focused

### ✅ 5. Proof Sweep
- Cursive stats integrated naturally (70% visitor ID, 220M+ consumer profiles, 140M+ business profiles, 450B+ intent signals, 200+ integrations)
- Specific Cursive features mentioned (Visitor Identification, Audience Builder, AI Studio, Intent Audiences)
- Customer success context added where appropriate
- Integration capabilities highlighted

### ✅ 6. Scan Sweep
- H2 every 300-400 words for readability
- H3 for sub-points within sections
- Long paragraphs converted to bullets
- Key takeaways bolded
- Lists formatted consistently

### ✅ 7. CTA Sweep
- Varied CTAs throughout each post
- Primary CTA: "Book a demo" with cal.com link
- Benefit-focused CTAs ("Ready to turn visitor behavior into revenue?")
- Specific solution page links where relevant
- Not repetitive across posts

### ✅ 8. One More Time Sweep (Final Review)
- Flow checked for readability
- Verified no "Retarget IQ" references remain
- Brand voice consistency confirmed
- Natural Cursive integration verified
