## What B2C Database Providers Actually Do

B2C database providers specialize in identifying anonymous shoppers and enriching customer records with verified consumer data.

### 1. Identify Anonymous Website Visitors

The primary capability is revealing who's shopping on your site even when they don't create accounts or fill out forms.

**Identification methods:**

- **Email matching:** Recognize visitors who previously provided email addresses

- **Data append services:** Match IP addresses and device IDs to consumer databases

**What you learn:**

- Name and contact information (email, phone)
- Demographic profiles (age, gender, location, household income)
- Purchase history and brand preferences
