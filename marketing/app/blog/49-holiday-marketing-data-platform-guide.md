### 3. Segment Holiday Audiences for Precision Targeting

Generic holiday promotions waste budget. Different customer segments need different messaging.

- **Messaging:** Welcome offers, brand education, free shipping

- **Profile:** Made purchases in previous Q4s or earlier this year

### Dormant Customers

- **Profile:** Purchased previously but haven't engaged in 6+ months
