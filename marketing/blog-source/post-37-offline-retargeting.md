## What is Offline Retargeting?

Offline retargeting means using physical channels (direct mail, phone calls, events, sales visits) to re-engage people who showed digital intent but didn't convert online.

**The process:**
1. Identify website visitors using visitor identification technology
2. Enrich with contact data (name, company, mailing address, phone)
3. Trigger offline outreach based on digital behavior
4. Coordinate offline and digital touches for maximum impact

**Results:** 20-30% connect rate, 15-20% meeting booking rate from connected calls.

## Common Offline Retargeting Mistakes

### Sending Generic, Unpersonalized Mail

**The mistake:** "Dear Valued Customer" messages that feel like junk mail.

**The fix:** Personalize with name, company, and specific behavior. "Hi Sarah, I noticed you viewed our Enterprise pricing page three times this week..."

### Not Coordinating with Digital Channels

**The mistake:** Direct mail operates in isolation from digital campaigns.

**The fix:** Coordinate timing and messaging. Digital ads reference direct mail. Email follows up on postcard. Everything works together.

### Poor Mail Quality

**The mistake:** Cheap paper, bad design, fuzzy images. Looks like spam.

**The fix:** Invest in quality. Premium cardstock, professional design, high-res images. Your mail quality reflects your brand quality.

### No Follow-Up

**The mistake:** Send one postcard, hope for response, move on.

**The fix:** Direct mail is first touch. Follow up with email, phone call, or another piece. Multi-touch sequences convert 3-5x better.

### Mailing to Unqualified Prospects

**The mistake:** Sending expensive direct mail to everyone who visits your blog.

**The fix:** Filter for high-intent (pricing page), high-fit (company size/industry), and high-value (large deal potential) before mailing.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### How does offline retargeting work?

Offline retargeting identifies website visitors using visitor identification technology, enriches them with contact data (mailing address, phone number), and triggers physical outreach (direct mail, phone calls) based on digital behavior. It bridges digital intent signals with offline touchpoints.

### What's the conversion rate for direct mail retargeting?

Direct mail to identified website visitors typically converts at 5-9%, compared to 1-2% for digital retargeting ads and 2-3% for email. When combined with coordinated digital campaigns, conversion rates reach 10-15%.

Postcard costs range from $0.50-$1.20 per piece including printing and postage. Letters cost $1.50-$3.00. Premium dimensional mail costs $5-$15. For typical B2B campaigns sending 1,000 pieces/month, expect $800-$1,200 in costs.

Yes, when done properly. Use confirmed business addresses, honor opt-out requests immediately, and maintain suppression lists. Partner with compliant platforms and consult legal counsel for specific requirements in your jurisdiction.

### How long does it take for direct mail to arrive?

Standard USPS delivery is 3-7 days depending on location. With automated direct mail platforms like Cursive, mail is typically triggered within 24 hours of the website visit and arrives within 5 days total.

### Can I retarget anonymous website visitors with direct mail?

### What industries benefit most from offline retargeting?

### Should I use direct mail for all website visitors?

No. Filter for high-intent (pricing page viewers), high-fit (matches ICP), and high-value (large potential deal size) before investing in direct mail. Use cheaper channels (email, digital ads) for broader audiences.
