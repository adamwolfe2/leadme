## Seven Sweeps Framework Applied

### Sweep 1: Clarity
- Removed vague Retarget IQ references
- Replaced generic "we" statements with specific Cursive capabilities
- Clarified technical concepts (visitor identification, intent signals, data enrichment)
- Simplified complex sentences and removed jargon

### Sweep 2: Voice & Tone
- Applied Cursive's "clear over clever" voice
- Used conversational but professional tone
- Maintained consistent second-person ("you") throughout
- Removed marketing fluff like "seamless," "robust," "game-changing"

### Sweep 3: So What
- Connected every feature to concrete benefits
- Added "which means" bridges (e.g., "70% identification rate means you stop guessing and start converting")
- Answered "why should I care" for each capability
- Focused on outcomes, not just features

### Sweep 4: Prove It
- Integrated key Cursive stats throughout:
  - 220M+ consumer profiles
  - 140M+ business profiles
  - 450B+ monthly intent signals
  - 30,000+ intent categories
  - 70% visitor identification rate
  - 200+ integrations
- Used specific numbers instead of vague claims
- Removed unsubstantiated competitor comparisons

### Sweep 5: Specificity
- Replaced "improve campaigns" with "identify 70% of anonymous visitors"
- Changed "save time" to "build audience segments in minutes"
- Updated "many customers" to concrete use cases
- Added specific timeframes (5-minute pixel install, 60-90 day ROI)

### Sweep 6: Heightened Emotion
- Addressed pain points: "Up to 70% of your B2B traffic never fills out a form"
- Created urgency: "When interest is fresh" / "while prospects are still researching"
- Painted before/after: "Stop guessing and start converting"
- Used questions to create reflection: "Can you see your hottest leads in under 10 seconds?"

### Sweep 7: Zero Risk
- Added comprehensive FAQ section (7 questions)
- Included clear CTA to demo booking
- Addressed objections (privacy, ROI timeline, implementation time)
- Provided specific next steps
